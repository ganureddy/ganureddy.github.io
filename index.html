About Me

The Person Behind the Code

I'm Ganu Bontalwad, a Software Developer and Product Manager based in Hyderabad, India. With over 5 years of hands-on experience, I specialize in building enterprise-grade ERP systems that power businesses across the globe.

My journey started with a deep curiosity for how software can transform business operations. Today, I lead product development at Physics Wallah — India's largest EdTech platform with 50M+ students — where I drive ERP product strategy, feature launches, and cross-functional execution at scale.

When I'm not coding or managing products, I'm exploring AI-powered ERP automation, building RAG pipelines, and finding new ways to make enterprise software smarter.
